Assignment 1

Reflection

Compulsory Assignment 1 focus on individual work focusing on BEM methodology. On this assignment I originally worked with Mia Sanne-Larsen. There were a lot of good BEM but also some issues and potential improvements.

Good aspects:

  1. Consistent BEM Naming: Our follows a consistent BEM naming convention. Blocks, elements, and modifiers are clearly defined.
  2. Clear Structure: The BEM methodology helps in maintaining a clear structure, making the HTML easier to read and the CSS easier to manage.

Issues

  1. Incorrect Modifiers: The modifier section--foryou should be part of a block name rather than a section element itself. It might be better named as section__foryou.
  2. Duplicate IDs: The page--login HTML seems to be accidentally included within the card-marked article, causing a mix-up of unrelated sections and elements.
  3. Unclosed Tags: There are unclosed tags, e.g., the li tag for Bil under the nav-marked list.
